Rules and Regulations for ICA Children Cultural Fest 2025

 

General Rules

ICA Children Cultural Fest 2025 will be conducted on 16th February 2025 (Jointly organised by Canberra CC Indian Activity Executive committee and Indian Cultural Association).

If registration exceeds maximum number of participants in any category, registration will be closed for those categories.  

If registration did not meet minimum number of participants in any categories, Organisers may cancel the competition of those categories.

There is no separate competition for girls and boys.

A participant can compete in a maximum of 2 Single stage items and group item(s) and 1 off stage item (Colouring/ Drawing/ Painting). Maximum 4 participation per participant

Registration will be confirmed only after payment of registration fees for each participating items.

The allotted time limit for each competition will be monitored by judges.

All decision by the judges and organizers are final.

Do not use any country’s national flag on stage other than Singapore’s.

All participants must carry age proof.

The Organisers reserve the right to cancel any of the event in case of any disputes without any prior notices.

Registration fee:

$10 per participant per competition item (stage & off-stage items)

Lunch coupon:

$8 for lunch coupon (Pre booking required)

 

Age Category

Age will be calculated based on 01/01/2025.

 

Individual Items

1) Below 8 years - Born on or after 01-01-2017

2) 8 to 10 years - Born on or after 01-01-2014 (01 Jan 2014 ~ 31 Dec 2016)

3) 11 to 14 years - Born on or after 01-01-2010 (01 Jan 2010 ~ 31 Dec 2013)

Group Items

1) 10 Years & Below - Born on or after 01-01-2014

2) 14 Years & below - Born on or after 01-01-2010

3) Below 18 years - Born on or after 01-01-2006

 

 

 

 

Colouring (Below 8 years)

Maximum time allowed is 40 min

Participants need to bring Crayons.

A picture will be provided for colouring.

Only one entry per participant is accepted.

Participants should not write name of the participants, entries must have Participant’s chest number

(Evaluation will be based on Originality, Colour mixing and Neatness)

Participant’s chest number.

Show & Tell (Below 8 years)

Maximum time allowed is 3 min.

Show & Tell should be only in English.

Mentioning of Participant's name and Reading is not allowed during Show & Tell.

There is no topic for this competition, but participants must bring the property.

(Evaluation will be based on Preparation & No stage fear, Clarity, Presentation and Flow of language)

Fancy dress (Below 8 years)

Max time allowed is 1 min

Participants are not allowed to use CD/Music track, but music from Phone/Tablet can be used through mic.

Participants are allowed to talk through mic, Language should be English.

Properties are allowed.

Do not use any country’s national flag on stage other than Singapore’s flag.

Make-up and costume must be arranged by participants.

(Evaluation will be based on Originality, Presentation on stage, Makeup and Costume)

 

Vocal solo music (8 to 10 years, 11 to 14 years)

Maximum time allowed is 4 min.

Karaoke is allowed.

Film songs or Semi Classical songs can be used.

Songs in any Indian language can be performed.

(Evaluation will be based on Voice, Overall Appeal, Rhythm, and Musical Pitch)

 

Cinematic, Semi Classical & Folk dance (Below 8 years & 8 to 10 years)
